    1 Dennis Ritchie C 1972 Ko'p paradigma: imperativ (protsessual), tuzilgan.

    4 Guido van Rossum Python 1991 Multi-paradigm: object-oriented, procedural (imperative), functional, structured, reflective.
    5 Rasmus Lerdorf PHP 1995 Ko'p paradigma: imperativ, funksional, ob'ektga yo'naltirilgan, protsessual, aks ettiruvchi.
